# Southern Eggnog

> Adapted from [this recipe](https://youtu.be/HYqXJWOZybY)

## Ingredients

* 2oz Burbon
* .5oz Brandy
* .5oz Maraschino Liquour
* .5oz Simple Syrup
* 1 whole egg

## Directions

1. Add ingredients to shaker tin. Seal and dry shake
1. Open shaker tin, add ice. Seal and reshake
1. Serve
